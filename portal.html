ASPIRE

Speaking in a relative way
You’ve been mad
As if you’ve been had
By the past
Years have come
The hope has gone
The courage always escaped you
nothing left to do but be face to face
Breathe, breathe in the air
Don’t, don’t be afraid
Afraid to care, to let go
Please leave, but don’t leave-me
You slip away, you hint there’s always more
You’re scared of heights, yet born to rise
Rise up past, by
the silhouettes of yourself
Wave goodbye to that child then
Say you know better, get some better friends
Wave goodbye to a former self
Yet when you’re alone,
whisper to the past
For tired advice
Fill your life with all you can touch and see
Maybe, If you close your eyes now
That’s what will always be?
Take away all those moments that make up a dog’s day?
Chasing tail,chewing bone, chasing your tail
Make the hours go by in the most hands-off way
Pacing around the same piece of ground
In your hometown, praying for somebody or something
To come and change your ways
Wind and smoke and cornered hopes
No choice but to believe that there’s something more
You stand tall in your childhood hall
And begin to begin again
